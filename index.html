How to Use Stripe Without a Bank Account

You can get paid through Stripe even if you don’t have a traditional bank account. Here’s how:

Step 1: Create a Payoneer Account

  • Go to payoneer.com and sign up
  • Verify your ID (passport or government ID)
  • Once approved, you get a virtual US bank account

Step 2: Sign Up on Stripe

  • Go to stripe.com and create an account
  • When asked for a bank account, input your Payoneer US bank details
  • Stripe accepts Payoneer in most countries

Step 3: Start Accepting Payments

  • Use Stripe Checkout or connect with platforms like Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy
  • Sell digital goods, subscriptions, or services
  • Stripe will payout to your Payoneer balance weekly

Alternative Options

  • Wise (TransferWise): Also offers virtual bank accounts
  • Gumroad: Lets you sell with PayPal or direct card payments
  • Crypto Payments: Use Coinbase Commerce, BTCPay, or direct wallets

FAQ

Is this legal?

Yes — Stripe supports virtual bank accounts in most regions through Payoneer or Wise.

Can I withdraw money to cash?

Yes, you can use Payoneer's card or transfer to a mobile wallet depending on your country.

What if I'm outside the U.S.?

Payoneer and Stripe are available in 100+ countries. Wise is a great alternative too.
